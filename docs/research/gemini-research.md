Section 3: The Anatomy of a High-Engagement Post
Crafting a single LinkedIn post that captures attention, delivers value, and drives action is both an art and a science. In a feed where users scroll at high speed, the structure and psychology of the post are as important as the message itself. This process involves four distinct stages: a scroll-stopping hook, a highly readable structure, a compelling narrative, and a clear call-to-action.
